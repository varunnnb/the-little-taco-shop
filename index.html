Taco Trivia

When did the tacos first appear in the United States?
Tacos first appeared in the United States in the early 1900s, with the first documented mention in 1905. This coincided with Mexican migrant workers moving to the Southwestern US to work on railroads, mines, and other jobs. They brought their culinary traditions, including tacos, with them. (Source: Smithsonian Magazine )
